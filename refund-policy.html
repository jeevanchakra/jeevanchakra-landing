Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: January 4, 2025

1. Success Fee Model

Jeevan Chakra charges a 5% success fee on monthly returns only. You are never charged upfront or monthly subscription fees.

Important: If you earn ₹0 returns in a month, you pay ₹0 fee.

2. No Setup or Exit Fees

  • Setup Fee: ₹0
  • Account Maintenance: ₹0
  • Exit Fee: ₹0 (You can close your account anytime)

3. Success Fee Refund Policy

Refunds Available:

  • Calculation Error: If we incorrectly calculate your returns and overcharge you, we will refund the excess amount within 7 business days.
  • Technical Error: If our platform malfunction causes incorrect fee deduction, full refund within 7 business days.

Refunds NOT Available:

  • Investment losses (market risk is inherent, we do not guarantee returns)
  • Correctly charged success fees (you agreed to 5% of returns)
  • Third-party charges (fund house fees, transaction charges by AMCs/platforms)

4. Investment Withdrawals

Jeevan Chakra does NOT hold your investment capital.

Your money is directly invested in:

  • Mutual funds (AMCs)
  • Government securities (RBI)
  • Corporate bonds (platforms like Wint Wealth)
  • P2P platforms (LenDenClub, etc.)

Withdrawal timelines depend on the instrument:

  • Mutual Funds: 1-3 business days
  • Bank FD: Instant (premature withdrawal may have penalties from bank)
  • P2P: As per platform terms (typically monthly/quarterly)
  • G-Secs/Bonds: Maturity-based (premature exit may not be possible)

5. Account Closure

You can close your Jeevan Chakra account anytime:

  • No exit penalty
  • Outstanding success fees (if any) will be settled before closure
  • Your investment holdings remain yours (we only provide advisory)
  • Account closure processed within 7 business days

6. Refund Process

To request a refund for fee overcharge:

  1. Email us at: ojhabanking@gmail.com
  2. Subject: "Refund Request - [Your Account ID]"
  3. Provide: Transaction details, reason, and proof
  4. We will review within 3 business days
  5. Approved refunds credited within 7 business days

7. Disputes

For unresolved disputes, you may approach:

  • SEBI (Securities and Exchange Board of India)
  • Consumer Courts under Consumer Protection Act

8. Important Disclaimer

Jeevan Chakra is an investment advisory platform, not a fund manager. We do not hold your money. Your capital is invested directly with regulated entities (AMCs, RBI, banks). We only advise and charge a success fee on returns generated.
