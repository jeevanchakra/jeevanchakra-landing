8. How We Protect Your Data

Security measures we implement:

8.1. Encryption: SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit; AES-256 encryption for data at rest.

8.2. Access Controls: Role-based access; only authorized personnel can access personal data.

8.3. Authentication: Multi-factor authentication (MFA) for account access.

8.4. Regular Audits: Security audits, vulnerability assessments, and penetration testing.

8.5. Employee Training: All staff trained on data protection and confidentiality.

8.6. Incident Response: Breach notification plan in place (72-hour notification under GDPR/DPDPA).

No System is 100% Secure: Despite our best efforts, no internet-based system is completely secure. You also have a responsibility to protect your password and account credentials.

9. How Long We Keep Your Data

Retention periods:

9.1. Active Account: As long as your account is active and for 7 years after closure (as mandated by SEBI/RBI).

9.2. KYC Documents: 7 years after account closure (PMLA Act requirement).

9.3. Transaction Records: 7 years (Income Tax Act, SEBI regulations).

9.4. Marketing Consent: Until you withdraw consent or 3 years of inactivity, whichever is earlier.

9.5. Website Analytics: 26 months (Google Analytics default).

After Retention Period: Data is securely deleted or anonymized so it cannot be linked back to you.
