At Jeevan Chakra, we are committed to providing excellent service and addressing your concerns promptly. This Grievance Redressal Policy outlines the process for filing complaints and our commitment to resolving them fairly and efficiently in compliance with SEBI and RBI guidelines.

1. Purpose & Scope

1.1. Purpose: To establish a transparent, fair, and efficient mechanism for resolving customer complaints and grievances.

1.2. Scope: This policy covers all complaints related to:

  • Investment advisory services provided by Jeevan Chakra
  • Account management and access issues
  • Billing, fees, and payment disputes
  • Data privacy and security concerns
  • Service quality and customer support
  • Unauthorized transactions or access
  • Mis-selling or unsuitable recommendations

This policy is designed in accordance with:

2.1. SEBI (Investment Advisers) Regulations, 2013 - Grievance redressal requirements

2.2. RBI Guidelines on Customer Service - Financial services customer protection

2.3. Consumer Protection Act, 2019 - Consumer rights and remedies

  • Difficulty accessing your account or platform issues

Financial Complaints:

  • Incorrect fee calculation or overcharging
  • Unauthorized deductions or transactions
  • Payment processing delays
  • Billing disputes

Advisory & Investment Complaints:

  • Unsuitable investment recommendations
  • Mis-selling or misleading information
  • Failure to disclose risks adequately
  • Unauthorized changes to your portfolio
  • Conflict of interest concerns

Data & Privacy Complaints:

  • Unauthorized use or disclosure of personal data
  • Data breach or security concerns

Conduct & Ethics Complaints:

  • Unprofessional behavior by staff
  • Discrimination or unfair treatment
  • Breach of confidentiality
  • Fraudulent activities or suspicious behavior

7. Possible Outcomes

After investigation, your complaint may be:

7.1. Resolved in Your Favor: We accept the complaint and implement corrective action (refund, compensation, service improvement).

7.2. Partially Resolved: We acknowledge some issues but cannot fully meet your demands (e.g., partial refund).

7.3. Not Upheld: After investigation, we find the complaint lacks merit or is outside our control (e.g., market loss complaints).

7.4. Withdrawn: You choose to withdraw the complaint.

Communication: In all cases, we will provide a detailed written explanation of our decision, the reasoning behind it, and your options for further escalation if you're not satisfied.

11. Frivolous or Vexatious Complaints

11.1. We reserve the right to decline or close complaints that are:

  • Clearly frivolous, malicious, or without merit
  • Repetitive complaints on already resolved issues
  • Abusive, threatening, or harassing in nature
  • Related to market losses (which are inherent risks, not service failures)

11.2. Even in such cases, we will provide a written explanation for closure.

12. Record Retention

12.1. All complaint records (emails, documents, resolution notes) are retained for 7 years as per SEBI/RBI guidelines.

12.2. Records are maintained securely and confidentially.

12.3. You have the right to request copies of your complaint records.

13. Learning from Complaints

13.1. Root Cause Analysis: We analyze complaint trends to identify systemic issues.

13.2. Process Improvements: Feedback from complaints drives policy and service improvements.

13.3. Staff Training: Recurring complaint themes are addressed through employee training.

13.4. Transparency: Quarterly complaint statistics published on our website.
