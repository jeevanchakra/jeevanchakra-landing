4.1. You have the right to revoke this consent at any time.

4.2. To opt-out, you must send a written request to ojhabanking@gmail.com with the subject line "REVOKE DND CONSENT".

4.3. We will process your revocation request within 7 business days. Communications related to critical account security or legal compliance cannot be opted out of.
